About EcoReclaim

🌍 Our Mission

EcoReclaim is dedicated to reducing e-waste and promoting responsible recycling. We aim to connect users with certified recycling centers to ensure safe disposal of electronic waste.

🔄 How It Works

Users can upload images of their e-waste, locate nearby recycling centers, and track their recycling impact.

📊 Our Impact

Over 500+ devices recycled, 1000+ users contributing to a greener planet!

👨‍💻 Our Team

We are a group of passionate developers, designers, and environmentalists committed to making a difference.
